My Competition Winning Cookies

Yes, not to brag, but I am a winner and yes there was a blue ribbon. I want to make it clear these are the full bodied recipes and will not be healthy, If you are looking for something healthy, get out, this is not for you.

With that in mind lets get to some baking.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup butter softened
  • 1 cup packed light brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups butterscotch chips
  • 2 cups roughly chopped pecans
  • 2/3 cups of toffee bits

A note on the toffee: I put mine through a colander to ensure there isnt any powder, but that is just personal preference
